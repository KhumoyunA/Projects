Welcome to College Planner!

Introduction

College Planner is designed by college students for making the busy schedule of students easily manageable.

Features

  1. Add Events: Ability to add events and information for them like title, category, description, start date and time, and end date and time
  2. Edit Events: Update the information of specific event
  3. Create To-Do List: Create a list for smaller task, and check off tasks once completed
  4. Calendar View: Full calendar view to see, manage, edit, filter events by specific date
